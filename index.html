About Me

Who am I

I'm a young Australian composer, orchestrator and arranger, with a passion for music for theatre and film. I recently graduated from The Royal College of Music, where I was a scholar supported by the Henry Wood Trust Award.

Experience

I have been lucky enough to have my compositions premiered in some great venues, such as the Sydney Opera House, The National Portrait Gallery, St Martin-in-the-Fields. I have also worked with the estate of film composer Michael Kamen, archiving his scores.
