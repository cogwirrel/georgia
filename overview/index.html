Georgia Scott

Hi I'm Georgia! I'm a freelance composer, orchestrator and arranger. I'm currently studying at the Sydney Conservatorium of Music, and graduated from The Royal College of Music, London in 2015.

Who am I

I'm a young Australian composer, orchestrator and arranger, with a passion for music, theatre and film. I am studying at the Syndey Conservatorium of Music as part of the Composing Women Program 2018/19, where I am supported by the Doris Burnett Ford scholarship. I graduated with an honours in Composition from The Royal College of Music, London in 2015.

Experience

I have been lucky enough to have my compositions premiered in some great venues, such as the Sydney Opera House, The National Portrait Gallery, St Martin-in-the-Fields. I was excited to work with Moorambilla Voices in 2017. In 2018/19 I'll be working with Claire Chase, the Sydney Chamber Opera and the Sydney Symphony Orchestra, both as a composer and orchestrator. I have also worked with the estate of film composer Michael Kamen, archiving his scores.
